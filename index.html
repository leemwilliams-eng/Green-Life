A Movement. Not Just An App.

We are informed and talented people.
We have gone to the moon and back.

But the signs of what we are doing are everywhere.
The straws. The packaging. The Styrofoam.

The food on the plate that traveled 3,000 miles
to get cold in front of you.
We walk past it every day because nobody
ever handed us a way to see it.

That changes now.

Green Life is not an app.
It is a way of seeing and a way of knowing.

Point your phone at the world around you.
We will tell you what you are looking at — honestly,
clearly, and without pretending to know more than we do.

Every picture throws a spark.
Every spark can start a fire.

Be present. Have knowledge. Pay attention.

Welcome to Green Life.
